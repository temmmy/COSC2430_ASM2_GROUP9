Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Here are some common questions and answers:

1. How do I place an order?

To place an order, follow these steps:

  • 1. Browse our product catalog.
  • 2. Click on the product you want to purchase.
  • 3. Add the product to your cart.
  • 4. Review your cart and proceed to checkout.
  • 5. Provide your shipping and payment information.
  • 6. Confirm your order.

2. How can I track my order?

You can track your order by logging into your account and navigating to the "Order History" section. There, you will find the tracking information for your order.
