Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to some common questions:

1. How do I place an order?

To place an order, simply browse our collection, select the vinyl records you want, and add them to your cart. Then, proceed to checkout, where you'll provide your shipping information and payment details. Once your order is confirmed, we'll process it and send you a confirmation email.

2. What is your return policy?

We want you to be satisfied with your purchase. If you're not completely happy with your order, please review our Return Policy for details on how to initiate a return or exchange.

3. How do I contact customer support?

Our customer support team is here to help. You can reach us by email at [email address] or by phone at [phone number]. We aim to respond to inquiries within [response time] during business hours.

Return Policy

We want you to love your vinyl records. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return or exchange it within [return period] days of receiving your order. Here's how:

1. Contact our customer support team to initiate the return process. You can reach us at [email address] or [phone number].

2. Pack the item securely in its original packaging, if possible.

3. Include a copy of your order confirmation or packing slip with your return.

4. Ship the item to the address provided by our customer support team.

5. Once we receive your return, we'll inspect it and process your refund or exchange.

Please note that returns must be in new and unused condition. Shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to a mistake on our part.
